April 24, 2017

InvestMN

InvestMN – Intelligent Investment Assistant for the Mongolian Stock Exchange

  • Read Details
  • Quick Tour
× InvestMN is a real-time analytics platform built on data scraped from the Mongolian Stock Exchange (MSE A).

Features:

  • Momentum Strategy Model: Signals optimal buy/sell times based on momentum patterns.
  • Portfolio Optimizer: Suggests optimal share allocation and ROI based on your investment and stock selections.
  • Stock Price Forecasting: Uses Fbprophet to predict prices, accounting for holidays and trading schedules.

Impact:
Designed for both beginner and experienced investors, InvestMN makes real-time stock analysis and portfolio planning accessible and actionable.

April 22, 2017

GoonTex

GoonTex – Discount Platform for University Students in Mongolia

  • Read Details
  • Quick Tour
× GoonTex is an application developed in 2022 to provide Mongolian university students with exclusive discount coupons for top brands, services, and retailers.

Features:

  • Targeted for Students: Verifies student status to unlock relevant discounts at popular venues.
  • Coupon Aggregation: Collects and curates discounts across food, clothing, electronics, and entertainment sectors.
  • Early Entrant: One of the first apps in Mongolia to digitally centralize and distribute student-specific promotions.

Impact:
GoonTex offered a modern solution to student affordability and fostering stronger brand to student relationships during its launch year.

Elite Scraper

Elite Scraper – Market Intelligence Tool for the Non-Banking Sector

  • Read Details
  • Quick Tour
× Elite Scraper is a specialized data scraping tool designed to support companies in the non-banking sector by collecting and structuring online market data from multiple industries.

Features:

  • Real Estate Listings Scraping: Gathers property listings, prices, and locations for housing market analysis.
  • Car and Electronics Market Data: Tracks second-hand prices, availability, and trends for consumer goods and vehicles.
  • Market Research Automation: Enables businesses to conduct competitor analysis and demand forecasting with fresh, high-volume datasets.

Impact:
By automating the data gathering process, Elite Scraper enables companies to focus on analysis and strategic decision-making, enhancing competitive edge in emerging Mongolian markets.
